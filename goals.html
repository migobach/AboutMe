Personal and Professional Goals.

Goals need to be explicit and evident in both our professional and personal lives. Whether pursuing goals related to personal hobbies or goals related to continuing to develop one's professional experience defined goals are essential for continued growth.

Professional

What is the dream work environment?

Continuing to grow professionally with a collaborative work community is an environment that Michael seeks. Michael recognizes that he is still starting out in his career and has much to learn. Being able to learn in a collaborative environment will benefit him professionally and personally. Ultimately, Michael views a restart in his career as an oppotunity to be molded into a professional developer. Finding an environment that will foster this growth through mentorship and collaboration will move Michael toward his goal of becoming a proficient, professional developer.

Personal

What is the dream?

Aren't we all looking to be happy and content? Isn't that all one could hope for? Seeking a balance between work and home is key to both happiness and contentment. Michael loves his hobbies and freetime; running, traveling, and creating are important facets of life that bring about balance and rejuvenation. Finding a work enviroment that allows for balance is a central goal to redefining a path forward for both Michael's career and life. Furthermore, being challenged to grow and learn is a a goal both personally and professionally.
